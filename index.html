Paste from Excel / accounting system

Ask the client to export the trial balance from their accounting software (SQL Account, AutoCount, UBS, Million, QuickBooks, Xero — they all export to Excel). Open it, copy the rows, paste here. Columns: Account, Debit, Credit and optionally Prior year. Signed single-amount format (Account, Amount, credits negative) also works. Prior-year figures: enter each balance as a positive number as presented in last year's signed FS (accumulated losses as a negative retained-earnings figure) — the engine checks that the comparative column articulates.
